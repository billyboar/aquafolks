This is a platform to allow aquarium hobbists to share their tanks and list details about them (listing what fishes they have and plants) and have a small marketplace people can share their stuffs or sell them. They can also sell their fishes or give away them.

People can comment on other people's tanks. Also people can share videos/blog posts about how they setup their tanks. Registered users can message other users. Marketplace should have a feature to display listings near them by miles/km radius.

Also people can show their active projects like: creates a project, adds images/video and text updates and results. Other people can subscribe to user or each individual projects and not necessarily following the users.
